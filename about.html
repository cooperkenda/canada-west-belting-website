About Us

40+ Years of Operations

Western Canada's best conveyor belt solutions.

Canada West Belting Ltd. was incorporated in 1994 to provide the industry with a source where the client could receive quality, professionalism and the experience needed for any scope of work that applies to conveyors and the applications required regarding them. We have division's in both BC and Alberta that services the entirety of Western Canada.

Our History

Canada West Belting has acquired extensive knowledge and experience through over 40+ years working in the belting industry. Now expanding its operations, Canada West Belting has opened a new division dedicated to the Alberta region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to best meet our clientele's needs through an extensive selection of products, exceptional customer service, and a safety-first mindset. Each work order is set up for success, with individuals who show up fully equipped to tackle any and all belting-related problems.

Our Experience

Our team of tradesmen, labourer's and management have extensive experience working in sawmills, grain terminals, aggregate, mines and more. Some of our clients include Dominion Diamond Mines ULC, Courer Silvertip Mine, Terrane Metals, Rio Tinto and Imperial Metals.

Your single source for conveyor belt solutions and services, located in Western Canada.
